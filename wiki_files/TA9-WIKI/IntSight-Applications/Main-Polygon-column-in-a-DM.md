1. Create a DM with a field that its role is 'Main Polygon'
![image.png](/.attachments/image-27cee2b5-6202-42f1-bc55-c6ff4e8dba3d.png)
2. enter a polygon value in the next format to the data where the field role is 'Main Polygon'
POLYGON((35.51456568164057 32.018745766955746,35.88672754687495 32.03271724556372,35.8839809648437 31.755218869778346,35.5269253007812 31.758721949220046,35.51456568164057 32.018745766955746))
##It is important to have spaces ' ' between the longitude and the latitude values
![image.png](/.attachments/image-c7cc0360-898c-498f-b1e4-ef89a7f429fb.png)

3. Go to the map skin of your DM and the Polygon will be visible
