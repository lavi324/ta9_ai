1. Open a new folder (Choose red color for quick identification)
![image.png](/.attachments/image-a36128de-1994-4a70-9ec4-c365968b0aa4.png)

2. Go to "Manage Access":
![image.png](/.attachments/image-6717f0e0-b669-4ce9-be28-c84dfa1fd271.png)

3. Go To "Advance Settings"
![image.png](/.attachments/image-219776ed-d5b3-42ec-9803-707650695b43.png)

4. Click on "Stop inheriting permissions" and click OK
![image.png](/.attachments/image-b28f0ae3-c7fc-4ce9-8bc4-ea708951b236.png)

5. Click on "Grant Permissions"
![image.png](/.attachments/image-cde81ab3-d6d3-4eaf-bc47-378248848b35.png)

6. Choose the specifc users from the organizations you want to gain access to the folder and click "Share":
![image.png](/.attachments/image-be072d20-4379-45d1-9a7a-627b778c8e08.png)

7. Remove all major group permissions:
![image.png](/.attachments/image-db0be536-f452-4650-8fc6-604e52d2d296.png)


8. Test with the person, ensure access was granted. Should receive a mail invite to the folder, and it should appear in the SharPoint folders space.
