1.Go to Data models, and choose “**Top Connections**”
 ![image.png](/.attachments/image-7b019714-103f-419a-a0ca-1ced10caa130.png)
2.Choose “**telephone**” identifier, and insert a **phone number**
 ![image.png](/.attachments/image-244e138b-a1eb-4da9-aa59-621c9afd52d8.png)
3.**Run** the query to get the top 10 connections.

4.Optional:
a.Use “**date range**” filter from the advanced filters
b.View the results in a link analysis skin
![image.png](/.attachments/image-7b563078-f300-4d18-8702-40fa015788a9.png)
c.In case you need less than 10 connections, use the “limit” filter to receive custom number od results between 1-10 (pick only one per query)
