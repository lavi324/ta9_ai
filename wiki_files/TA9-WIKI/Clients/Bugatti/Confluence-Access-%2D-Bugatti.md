# **Connecting to Confluence:**
1. Go to this link for general Constellation:
https://constellation.soprasteria.com/dashboard/

2. Enter your credentials (PM is responsible for the credentials, advise: anthony.erokhin@soprasteria.com)

*  If its your first time connecting, you should be redirected to another page for password renewal.  Follow the instructions, as the password should be in a specific format.






# **How to change Partner (Confluence) Password:**

To change your Partner password: 

1. Log in to the Partners Portal: https://partners.soprasteria.com

2. Click on Preferences / General / Change your password
