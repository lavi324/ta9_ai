2. An image of the **Israeli ID + "ספח"**

Scan them and send to the PM in email.

Set a date with Lena/Neta-lee to arrive to the interview, in the address: "**אבא הילל סילבר 18, לוד**"
You need to bring with you:
1. The **original files** you filled.
2. Your **Israeli ID**.
3. Your Israelis **Passport** (and foreign if you have one).
