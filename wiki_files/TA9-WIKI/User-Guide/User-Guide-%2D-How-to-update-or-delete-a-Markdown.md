>**Note:** For managing markdowns - you need the TA9 Markdown manager user - credentials can be found on passbolt: 

Here is a detailed wiki on how to upload content to the online user guide on our website (https://www.t-a9.com/)

First, access the "user guide" section on the site:

![AS 37.png](/.attachments/AS%2037-9ccf1c32-af99-44d0-bcf3-be9a0c4cd7eb.png)

Sign in with your manager credentials in the login page:

![AS 38.png](/.attachments/AS%2038-12ebee27-4f66-4018-8c69-0a5dc16fade0.png)

Hover over the new button on the top left side of your screen and click the "dashboard" button:

![AS 42.png](/.attachments/AS%2042-8bb54a76-76d4-452b-9445-88a10cee746f.png)

Once you have entered the dashboard, hover over the "Markdown" button and click on that section:
![AS 43.png](/.attachments/AS%2043-520f19e5-7d5e-4e2b-a68b-78822ff572df.png)

Click the edit button the edit the markdown:
![AS 44.png](/.attachments/AS%2044-9ba9f63a-9440-4351-898f-7df50083895e.png)

Update the text and select the blue update button:
![AS 47.png](/.attachments/AS%2047-408ecc21-bf46-4089-afe0-15cbcd8ac488.png)

Return to the main menu and view your updated markdown:

![AS 48.png](/.attachments/AS%2048-65793d35-7873-4f69-88b8-451135b4f84c.png)
