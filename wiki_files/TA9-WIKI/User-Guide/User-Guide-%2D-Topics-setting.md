Here is a detailed wiki on how to upload content to the online user guide on our website (https://www.t-a9.com/)

First, access the "user guide" section on the site:

![AS 37.png](/.attachments/AS%2037-9ccf1c32-af99-44d0-bcf3-be9a0c4cd7eb.png)
 
Sign in with your manager credentials in the login page:

![AS 38.png](/.attachments/AS%2038-12ebee27-4f66-4018-8c69-0a5dc16fade0.png)

Hover over the new button on the top left side of your screen and click "Markdown":

![AS 39.png](/.attachments/AS%2039-353c58c0-1140-40f4-b407-e8e1bc2887bb.png)

Fill in the details, and make sure to select a parent term if you wish to assign the new topic to another main topic:

Click "add a new topic" the details and go back the the main screen in order to view it.
