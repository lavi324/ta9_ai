Open the orient DB and click on Schema. 
Then click on the entity you want to add an index to:

![image.png](/.attachments/image-c557231f-3345-4324-ad6c-6baec3e2488a.png)

Scroll down to the Indexes and click on New Index:

![image.png](/.attachments/image-781ea22e-9e37-43af-a85c-02cfc643ef33.png)


Select the relevant field, type and engine properties:

![image.png](/.attachments/image-65c4a04d-6afa-4c18-9e8b-524f7490e1bd.png)

![image.png](/.attachments/image-1799966c-c108-4bc7-831a-abde62b36d0d.png)

Click save
The index will automatically run on the selected field.
