This is a way to block the access to the internet but still to success login to the system.
This way allows to test the system on environment without internet.   

# How to block the internet

1.Go to control panel -> Network and Internet.
 
2.Click on Network and Sharing Center.

3.Click on Change adapter settings.

4.Right click on the Wi-Fi you want to connect to and click on properties.

5.Click on internet protocol Version 4 (TCP/IPv4) and click on properties.
